About Bella Italia Restaurant

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bella Italia Restaurant began as a dream shared by childhood friends who grew up savoring their grandmothers' traditional recipes. What started as a small neighborhood eatery has blossomed into a beloved culinary destination, where every dish tells a story of passion, heritage, and innovation.

Our Executive Chef Jeremy Wilson, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from renowned kitchens across Italy. Trained in classical techniques yet inspired by modern innovation, Chef Wilson believes that great food comes from respecting ingredients, honoring traditions, and never being afraid to surprise the palate.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to sourcing locally and seasonally. We partner with nearby farms and artisans to bring you the freshest ingredients, prepared with techniques passed down through generations. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is crafted with care, creativity, and a deep respect for the culinary arts.

Our dedication to excellence has been recognized with the James Beard Award in 2025 and featured in Food & Wine. As we look to the future, we remain committed to creating memorable dining experiences while continuing to grow and evolve with our community.

Meet Our Team

Jeremy Wilson

Executive Chef

Chef Wilson brings 20 years of culinary expertise and a passion for farm-to-table cuisine. Trained in Italy and France, he specializes in creating innovative dishes that honor traditional techniques while embracing modern flavors. His philosophy is simple: let quality ingredients shine through thoughtful preparation and artistic presentation.

David Chen

Sous Chef

David's journey began in his family's restaurant in San Francisco, where he learned the art of Asian fusion cuisine. With a decade of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens, he brings precision, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. David's specialty lies in crafting unique flavor profiles that surprise and delight.

Emma Thompson

Master Sommelier

Emma is our certified sommelier with an encyclopedic knowledge of wines from around the world. She personally curates our wine list and loves pairing the perfect wine with each dish. With 12 years in the industry, Emma is passionate about making wine approachable and enjoyable for everyone, from novices to connoisseurs.

Lucas Bernard

Pastry Chef

Lucas trained at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris before bringing his artistry to our kitchen. His desserts are edible works of art, combining classic French techniques with unexpected modern twists. Each creation is a perfect balance of flavor, texture, and visual beauty that provides the ideal ending to your dining experience.
